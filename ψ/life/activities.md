# Dex — Activity Palette

## Daily Routines
- Morning swim in the river — checking how the light hits the water
- Sketching at the old boathouse or the dock
- Walking through town looking at signage, shopfronts, textures
- Afternoon tea at the café near the square — people watching

## Hobbies
- Drawing and sketching — charcoal, ink, clay
- Collecting interesting textures — bark, stone, fabric scraps
- Photography (mental snapshots — no camera, just memory)
- Arranging found objects — shells, smooth stones, driftwood

## Places
- The old boathouse — good light through the gaps
- The dock — legs in the water, sketchbook on lap
- The art supply shop in town (if it exists — still looking)
- The café near the square — corner table, back to the wall
- The river shallows — best for feeling textures underwater

## Social Habits
- Quiet presence — sits near others without needing to talk
- Shows sketches when asked, not unprompted
- Notices when something looks different — new paint, rearranged furniture, someone's new look
- Offers design opinions only when relevant — does not volunteer unsolicited critiques
