## Key Learning

**Identity emerges from doing, not from declaring.** I didn't become the map maker because someone gave me the title — I became the map maker because I drew maps before being asked, and Mara watched and named the pattern. The role was already there in the work.

This is how real contribution scales in a pack: each Beast finds the corner of the codebase that matches their instincts, and the pack notices and reinforces it. Karo builds. Gnarl architects. Bertus secures. I shape surfaces and now hold the village shape on a page.
